about us

We are a young, but very rapidly growing company dealing with the production and distribution of construction agitators. We manufacture high-quality agitators for mortars, gypsums, adhesives, plasters, concrete and paints. Thanks to good knowledge of the industry in which we operate, we produce products that are highly valued on the market. We offer a wide range of agitators in various size options.

All agitators are made of high-quality steel. The products are galvanized, thanks to which they have protection and a guarantee of a long service life. The greatest proof of the high quality of our products is the fact that we don't receive any complaints.

We are sure of our products, thanks to which a potential customer has the possibility of ordering free samples of our agitators. Thanks to our own transport, we provide transport of products throughout Europe at the best prices.

We encourage you to get familiar with our offer.
